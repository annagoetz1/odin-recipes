Lasagna Recipe

World's Best Lasagna!

This recipe is gushed about every time I take it to a gathering with family or friends. Youur family and friends will love this recipe too!

With simple ingredients, quick cooking, and raving reviews, this will be a recipe to add to your cookbook for the long-haul!

Ingredients

  • ricotta
  • parmesan
  • lasagna noodles
  • basil
  • marinara sauce
  • ground beef

Directions

  1. Cook lasagna noodles until slightly al dente
  2. Prepare cooking dish with oil to grease it
  3. Layer noodles, sauce, beef and cheese in any order you would like until the dish is almost filled to the top
  4. bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes
