Meatloaf Recipe

If you need to clear out your fridge, or don't feel like standing in front of your stove cooking for hours on end, this recipe is the best mix, dump-and-go recipe!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of 80/20 ground beef
  • 1/2 cup of breadcrumbs
  • 2 tablespoons of tomato paste
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 3 tablespoons of italian seasoning
  • 1/4 cup of grated onion

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375 degrees
  2. Grate your onion using a cheese grater, measuring out a 1/4 cup
  3. After finishing your onion and beating your 1 egg, mix all of the ingredients together throoughly
  4. Grease a 9 by 13 pan and pour the mixture in, using your hand or a spatula to even out the mixture
  5. Cook for 45 miunutes and serve immediately. You can make a barbecue or ketchup sauce for topping while it cooks, and top the loaf with it after coming out of the oven
